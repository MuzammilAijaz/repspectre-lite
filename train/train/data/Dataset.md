# Problem based dataset collection
==========================================================================================

## Lift
Common problems
- person goes very slow in the middle of the lift but still manages to complete the lift
- person lifts it fast because it was easy
- person lifts it slow because its hard / near max
- From the Floor Lifts

Lift Specific Problems
  - Vertical Press (from upper chest to above)

  - Horizontal Press

  - Barbell on the Traps

  - From Waist

## No Lift (Noise data)
Contains sitation for each lift, where the action SHOULD NOT be classified as a lift

Common Noise
- person moves the barbell around for whatever reason

Lift Specific Noise
  - From the Floor Lifts
    - barbell rolls
    - person puts a plate on
    - person gives a slack pull before starting lift creating a spike/noise in the sensor
    - person drops the barbell from a height (to setup the lift?)

  - Vertical Press (from upper chest to above)
    - person lifts from rack that is shorter
    - person lifts from rack that is taller
    - person lifts from the ground to chest in order to setup for lift

  - Horizontal Press
    - person lifts from rack
    - person gets a spot to lift from the rack ?: should be similar to above

  - Barbell on the Traps
    - person lifts from rack smaller
    - person lifts from rack taller

  - From Waist
    - person lifts from from
    - person lifts from rack
